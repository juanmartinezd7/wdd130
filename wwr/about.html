Cowboy Rafting

Everything you are looking for on a Full Day Rafting trip, where you will experience the rapids. We will equip you with any necessary gear and a safety briefing from your expert rafting guide. 100% customer satisfaction.

Located in the heart of Texas, Cowboy Rafting has some of the finest whitewater rafting this side of the Lone Star State. During your whitewater rafting trip, expect Class II & III rapids.

History

Our guided rafting trips, canoe trips, guided hikes and backroad tours are structured to give you information about the region's history, geology, wildlife and plant life through the expertise of our knowledgeable guides.
